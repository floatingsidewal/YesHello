When YesHello Applies üéØ

Scenario: You want to ask a question but worry it's "dumb"
YesHello approach: Just ask. Lead with "quick question" or "hey!" if you want. I promise you're not bothering me.
Scenario: You're having a rough day and need to vent
YesHello approach: "Hey, got a minute?" is totally fine. You don't need to write a formal problem statement.

YesHello approach: Share it! Laughter is underrated in remote work. Start with "lol check this out" if you want.

YesHello approach: "Want to pair on something?" is a complete sentence. No need for a calendar invite with a formal agenda.

YesHello approach: "Morning! How was your weekend?" is perfectly valid and actually helps team morale.

YesHello approach: Ask anyway! "Hey, when you have a sec..." is totally fine. I'll respond when I can.

YesHello approach: Say so! "Hi, I'm struggling with X" is exactly what you should send. We all started somewhere.

YesHello approach: Do it! "Hey, thanks for your help earlier!" makes people's day. Gratitude is never wasted.
